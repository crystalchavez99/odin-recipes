Meatloaf

Description

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion,chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combined beef, egg, onion, and bread crumbs in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper, then place in a loaf pan to form loaf.
  3. Combine brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup seperately. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
